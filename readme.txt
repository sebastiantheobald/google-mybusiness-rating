This Plugins gets average rating from your company\'s Google My Business entry. You can display the rating on your wordpress website.

== Description ==
This Plugin offers you a possibility to show your Google My Business rating average on your website. Therefore you can use an shortcode to show a badge which includes the star-rating, the total amount of ratings and a numeric average rating.

== Installation ==
= Installation =
* Install using the WordPress built-in Plugin installer.
* Aktiviere das Plugin über den Menüpunkt ‚Plugins‘ in WordPress.

= Configuration =
* Go to Settings > Google My Business Rating
* paste a valid Google API Key which is allowed to use the Google Places API. You have to create the API Key via Google Cloud Platform
* paste your company\'s place id. Get place id here: [Google Places API](https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/places/web-service/place-id)
* paste URL to your Google My Business Review Form. To get the URL visit your Google My Business profile logged in as admin. Click the blue button at the top right \"Get more Reviews\". Copy the shorted URL.

= Show Rating =
to show your rating place the shortcode [gmb_rating_badge] to your site.
